🎯 What You'll Learn

Master UI/UX design from beginner to professional through a structured, project-based roadmap designed for modern digital creators.

8 Lessons • 45 min

02

Design Fundamentals

9 Lessons • 2 Hours

Build visual confidence with color theory, typography, layout, grid systems, hierarchy, and white space.

03

Figma Essentials

12 Lessons • 2 Hours

Master frames, components, auto layout, constraints, variants, plugins, and export workflows.

04

UX Research

7 Lessons • 1.5 Hours

5 Lessons • 1 Hour

7 Lessons • 2 Hours

6 Lessons • 1.5 Hours

6 Lessons • 1.5 Hours
